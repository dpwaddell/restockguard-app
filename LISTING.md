## Tagline

## Short Description (160 chars)
Automatically notify customers when out-of-stock products are back. Recover lost sales with customizable email alerts, waitlists, and preorder mode.

## Full Description

**Stop losing sales to out-of-stock products. RestockGuard automatically emails your customers the moment inventory is back — turning missed sales into recovered revenue.**

Every day, customers land on your store, find a product they want, and leave because it's out of stock. Without RestockGuard, that sale is gone forever. With RestockGuard, those customers sign up to be notified — and when you restock, they come back and buy.

### How It Works

1. **Install the widget** — add RestockGuard's lightweight snippet to your theme in minutes. No coding required. The "Notify me when back in stock" button appears automatically on out-of-stock product pages.

2. **Customers sign up** — shoppers enter their email address to join the waitlist for a specific product. RestockGuard handles storage, deduplication, and opt-in confirmation.

3. **Restock and recover** — the moment Shopify marks your inventory as available, RestockGuard automatically sends a beautifully branded email alert to every subscriber. Sit back and watch the orders roll in.

### Key Features

**Automated Back-in-Stock Alerts**
Powered by Shopify's inventory webhooks, RestockGuard fires alert emails the instant a product is restocked — no manual steps required. Supports product-level and variant-level notifications so customers get alerted for exactly the size, colour, or style they want.

**Waitlist Management**
View and export your full subscriber list from the dashboard. Filter by product, status, or date. See exactly how many customers are waiting for each SKU — invaluable for purchasing decisions.

**Preorder Mode**
Not restocked yet but confident it's coming? Enable Preorder Mode on any product to let customers complete a real Shopify checkout and reserve their item before it arrives. Set a ships-by date to set expectations and reduce support queries.

**Fully Customisable Widget**
Match the widget to your brand with custom button text, colours, email sender name, and success messages. No developer needed — everything is configurable from the RestockGuard settings page.

**Analytics Dashboard**
Track subscribers, alerts sent, open rates, click rates, and — most importantly — recovered revenue. See which products are most in-demand before you've even restocked them.

**GDPR & Privacy Ready**
RestockGuard is fully compliant with Shopify's GDPR requirements. All three mandatory webhooks (customer data request, customer redact, shop redact) are implemented and tested. Subscribers can unsubscribe with one click from every email.
